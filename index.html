3 · Preview & export

Export note: recording happens in real time (as long as the video). Output is MP4 on iPhone/Safari, WebM on most Android/Chrome browsers. Instagram prefers MP4 — if you get a WebM, convert it (e.g. CloudConvert) or run the export on an iPhone. Keep this tab in front while exporting.
